Map Information

Haven is the first of 2 Triple Bombsite map , it also has a mid Area unlike its counterpart, Lotus. All 4 Agents we talk about are good on this Map.

put yourself into the position seen on the Picture and look at the lowest part of the branch, aim at that and do a 1 bar no bounce shot.
For A fast pushes this is really useful to clear a long and help your team not get early peaked

For this lineup you need to align yourself with the first of the "windows" seen in the picture, sneak and aim at the top part of the house on the left side of long, do a one bounce full charge.
This clears out the entirity of C site.
