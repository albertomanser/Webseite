Map Information

Lotus is the newest map and has 3 Bombsites. The attackers spawn in a very small area which insetivises defensiv Agression to prevent the Attackers from getting more Space. Viper, Kayo and Sova see a lot of play here. For sova you can mostly throw you recondarts on walls, we will only show 1 lineup.

For this lineup you will see a orange tree in c main, aim at that tree and do a full charge no bounce, this will show any attackers pushing c.
