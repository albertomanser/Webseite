Map Information

Ascent is a Map with 2 Bombsites and a middle area, it has both open areas and closed off areas and is widely regarded as one of the better Maps of Valorant. Fade, Sova and Kayo see alot of play here.

In A Main, walk into the corner on the right hand green box seen in this picture, aim at the antenna in the top right and move your crosshair to the left a bit. Shoot with 1 bar no bounces.
This Lineup clears almost the entire A site and lands really fast which is very helpful for a fast push

In B Lobby, walk into the Barrier and aim at the tree you see in the window. shoot with a full charge no bounces.
This Lineup is really easy and clears site and stairs for a fast B site take.
