Map Information

Fracture is a unique map because it has 2 areas which are available for Attackers before the round starts. Because of this defenders need to constantly attack some space to not get overrun.

For this lineup you need to stand where the knife is in this picture, aim at the border between the vegetation and the wall of the second wall segment and do a full charge 1 bounce
This will clear site.

This is a defens side lineup, on b stand in the corner of site which you can see on the right side of the picture, line up the bottom part of you charge bar with the greenery of the wall and do a one charge. This will give you some early information.
